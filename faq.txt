-> How does it work?
    To access to this service you should do the following steps:
      - Create an account 
      - Regist your pet(s)
      - Make a request for a petsitter

-> Can I choose the Petsitter that I want to hire?
    Absolutely yes! But it will depend on them availability.

-> How does the payment process work?
    Pre-payment of 50% of the service price via MB way or bank transfer.

-> Are the petsitters qualified?
    Yes! They will do their best to take care of your pet supplying all their needs.

-> Can I meet the Petsitter before hiring?
    Yes. We encourage you to meet each other previously and introduce the animals.
    When you contact the petsitter you can schedule a visit or previous meeting.

-> I choosed the Petsitter but I didn't get the contact.
    You need to make the pre-payment first.

-> Do you have a table price?
    No. The prices are established by each Petsitter, so the prices can be variable according to each petsitter. 

-> Will I get refunded if I cancel the service?
    Only if you cancel the service with 7 or more days in advance.
    In order to refund you, we emit a credit of 50% of the total amount pre-payed and you can use it in the next service.

-> Does the prices include the feeding?
    No. The food and all the materials needed to the maintenance of the animals should be provided by the owner.
